Research

Biography

Dr. Asad H. Sahir is a Chemical Engineering Educator with research interests in Process Systems Engineering (Modeling, Control, and Optimization), Energy and Environmental Engineering. His previous professional experience includes working with the USDOE's National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) as a Postdoctoral Researcher-Chemical Process Engineer; and as a Senior Research Engineer with Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL)’s Corporate Research and Development Centre. He pursued his doctoral research at the University of Utah under the guidance of Prof. JoAnn S. Lighty and Prof. Adel Sarofim (deceased). From November 2017 to July 2021, Dr. Sahir served as the Head-Professional Development, Career Development and Corporate Relations Centre, IIT Ropar and has been the advisor for the Society of Women Engineers IIT Ropar Affiliate. He works extensively with industrial partners and governmental organizations on research and engineering education projects.
