Research

Biography

"I finished my B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering from IIT Delhi and then did my M.Sc.(Engg.) and Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from IISc. Bangalore. After that I finished my Post Doctoral research in Laboratory of Cell Physics, I.S.I.S., University of Strasbourg, France. I joined IIT Ropar in 2013 as a faculty in School of Mechanical, Materials and Energy Engineering and later shifted to newly established Department of Chemical Engineering in 2017."
